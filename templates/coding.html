COMPLAINCE MOLE

Languages: Python, Pandas, Matplotlib

Meet Compliance Mole 🧪 — a Python-powered app that checks the concentration, pH level, temperature, pressure, and flow rate for various chemical samples to see if a company passes a compliance test. If a sample passes, great! ✅ If not, Compliance Mole sniffs it out!👃

MK:delta

Language: Python / Pandas

∆ Meet MK:Delta 🐍 A Python-powered app that generates annual True-Up price estimates for XaaS-based License Agreements!
💡 Background / Inspiration:
💼 In a previous role in the world of IT Distribution, I specialized in Cisco XaaS / License Agreement sales operations. At the time, the Cisco Business Unit didn’t have a dedicated team for subscription management — leaving a need for process standardization.
🛠 I took the challenge head-on, applying LEAN methodology and collaborating across teams to begin creating harmony between internal systems and the many nuances of XaaS subscriptions.
🧩 One nuance in particular caught my attention: Cisco’s True Forward process. As Enterprise Agreement (EA) sales accelerated, estimating True Forward costs became increasingly complex. Many partners weren’t familiar with how True Forwards worked, and internal quoting tools couldn’t handle the Cisco report formats.
✨ This process had a certain je ne sais quoi that fascinated me. So, I went down the rabbit hole to learn every detail of the calculations and workflows involved.
⚙ The Project:
💻 This project began as a Visual Basic macro I built as a personal side project on my own time, using Microsoft Copilot to generate 100% fictional sample reports. My goal was to surprise my previous company with a working solution; however, I separated from the company prior to the big reveal.
🧠 After leaving, inspired by Lex Fridman, I threw myself into the world of Python! Looking for real-life problems to solve, I circled back to the True-Up pricing challenge…
🕺And that’s how MK:Delta was born 💥

⚠ Important Notes
✅ MK: Delta was developed on my personal computer as a side project.
✅ Tested only with synthetic / randomly generated data and reports — no real customer, distributor, partner, or Cisco data or reports were used.
✅ All companies and products in the reports are 100% fictional.
✅ Inspired by Cisco’s True Forward process, but not affiliated with or endorsed by Cisco (or any other company). This is a demo / proof-of-concept, not an official Cisco tool.
